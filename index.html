Day 3: October 15, 2025 - Cultural Wonders and City Views

Optimized Route: Hiyori Hotel Maihama → Sensō-ji Temple → Tokyo Skytree → Hiyori Hotel Maihama

Total Distance: 35.34 km | Estimated Travel Time: 1 hour 5 mins

⛩️ Morning (9:30 AM - 12:30 PM): Sensō-ji Temple

📍 Location: Sensō-ji Temple (35.714769, 139.796657) View Map

Begin your day with a visit to Sensō-ji Temple, Tokyo's oldest temple, located in the traditional Asakusa district. Walk through the impressive Kaminarimon (Thunder Gate) and Nakamise-dori, a bustling shopping street.

Why Visit: A fantastic cultural experience allowing exploration and appreciation of Japanese history in a lively setting that captures children's interest.

Activities:

  • Walk through Kaminarimon Gate and Nakamise-dori.
  • Explore the main hall and admire the architecture.
  • Participate in a traditional omikuji (fortune-telling) ritual.
  • Enjoy traditional Japanese snacks and treats.

Budget: Entry free. Souvenirs/snacks approx. ¥3,000 - ¥5,000.

Travel to Next Location: Sensō-ji Temple → Tokyo Skytree | 2.02 km | 5 mins (Taxi/Walk)

  • ⚠️ Be respectful of worshippers. Dress modestly. Keep a close eye on children in crowded areas.
  • ⏰ Arrive in the morning to avoid the biggest crowds.

🗼 Afternoon (1:30 PM - 4:30 PM): Tokyo Skytree

📍 Location: Tokyo Skytree (35.710046, 139.810477) View Map

Ascend to breathtaking heights at the Tokyo Skytree, Japan's tallest structure. From its observation decks, you'll be treated to panoramic views of the sprawling Tokyo cityscape, and on a clear day, Mount Fuji.

Why Visit: Offers an incredible perspective of Tokyo and is an exciting architectural marvel, providing a "wow" factor for children.

Activities:

  • Enjoy 360-degree views of Tokyo from the observation decks.
  • Explore the shops and restaurants at Tokyo Solamachi (at the base).
  • Take memorable family photos with the city as your backdrop.

Budget: Admission approx. ¥6,100 (family). Lunch approx. ¥5,000 - ¥8,000.

Travel to Next Location: Tokyo Skytree → Hiyori Hotel Maihama | 16.56 km | 35 mins (Taxi/Train)

  • ⚠️ Purchase tickets in advance online. Check the weather forecast as it affects visibility.
  • ⏰ Late afternoon for views of both daylight and sunset.

🍽️ Evening (6:30 PM - 8:30 PM): Dinner near Hotel / Hotel Amenities

📍 Location: Hiyori Hotel Maihama Area (35.646235, 139.8971) View Map

After a day of cultural exploration and high-altitude views, return to the Maihama area for a relaxed evening. You can dine at one of the many family-friendly restaurants nearby or at the hotel's own restaurant.

Why Visit: A relaxed evening near the hotel is perfect for recharging after a busy day, ensuring children get adequate rest.

Activities:

  • Enjoy a relaxed dinner with your family.
  • Allow children to unwind with light activities at the hotel.

Budget: Dinner cost approx. ¥5,000 - ¥8,000.

Day 4: October 16, 2025 - Animal Encounters and Green Spaces

Optimized Route: Hiyori Hotel Maihama → Ueno Zoo → Ueno Park → Hiyori Hotel Maihama

Total Distance: 40.82 km | Estimated Travel Time: 1 hour 10 mins

🐼 Morning (9:30 AM - 1:00 PM): Ueno Zoo

📍 Location: Ueno Zoo (35.7175, 139.7711) View Map

Delight your children with a visit to Ueno Zoo, Japan's oldest zoo. Home to a diverse range of animals, including giant pandas, elephants, and gorillas, it offers an engaging and educational experience.

Why Visit: A fantastic family-friendly attraction for both entertainment and education, allowing kids to see animals they might only have read about.

Activities:

  • Observe giant pandas and other exotic animals.
  • Ride the monorail between the East and West gardens.
  • Enjoy educational exhibits and feeding demonstrations.

Budget: Admission approx. ¥1,200 (adults). Lunch approx. ¥4,000 - ¥6,000.

Travel to Next Location: Ueno Zoo → Ueno Park | 0.86 km | 5 mins (Walk)

  • ⚠️ The zoo can get crowded; check the official website for opening hours.
  • ⏰ Arrive shortly after opening to avoid the largest crowds.

🌳 Afternoon (1:00 PM - 4:00 PM): Ueno Park

📍 Location: Ueno Park (35.7147, 139.7741) View Map

After your zoo adventure, explore the expansive Ueno Park, a cultural hub that also houses museums, shrines, and beautiful green spaces. Enjoy a leisurely stroll or a picnic.

Why Visit: Offers a blend of nature and culture, providing a relaxing yet engaging experience for the family and options for different interests.

Activities:

  • Enjoy a picnic lunch in the park.
  • Rent paddle boats on Shinobazu Pond (seasonal).
  • Visit shrines or explore the garden areas.

Budget: Free entry to park. Paddle boat rentals/museums extra.

Travel to Next Location: Ueno Park → Hiyori Hotel Maihama | 20.85 km | 35 mins (Taxi/Train)

  • 🎒 Picnic blanket, frisbee or ball for children recommended.
  • ⚠️ Check operating hours and admission fees for any museums.

Budget Tips:

  • Public Transportation: Utilize Tokyo's efficient public transportation system (trains and subways) with an IC card (Suica or Pasmo) to save on taxi fares. Consider a Tokyo Subway Ticket for unlimited rides.
  • Meal Deals: Look for family meal deals at restaurants, especially around theme parks and major attractions. Convenience stores also offer affordable and tasty meal options.
  • Free Attractions: Take advantage of Tokyo's many free attractions, such as parks, gardens, and walking through vibrant districts like Shibuya and Shinjuku.
  • Souvenir Savvy: Set a budget for souvenirs and encourage children to choose wisely. Many budget-friendly and unique souvenirs can be found at local markets.
  • Water Bottles: Carry reusable water bottles and refill them at public fountains or designated stations to avoid purchasing bottled water.

Travel Tips

  • 💡 Tokyo's public transport is excellent; consider a Pasmo/Suica card or a Tokyo Subway Ticket.
  • 💡 Japanese etiquette is important: bow slightly as a greeting, avoid eating or drinking on public transport.
  • 💡 Tipping is not customary in Japan and can sometimes be seen as rude.
  • 💡 Convenience stores (Konbini) like 7-Eleven, FamilyMart, and Lawson are great for affordable meals, snacks, and ATMs.
  • 💡 Free Wi-Fi can be found in many public places, hotels, and cafes, but a portable Wi-Fi device or local SIM card is recommended for consistent connectivity.
