Daily Itinerary

Day 1: 2025-10-14 - Shopping Spree & City Highlights

Optimized Route: Hotel Lounge Hotel → Phoenix Marketcity → Commercial Street → UB City Mall → Hotel Lounge Hotel

Total Day Distance: 28.5 km | Total Travel Time: 1 hour 45 minutes

Morning: Phoenix Marketcity

Phoenix Marketcity is one of Bengaluru's largest and most popular malls, offering a vast array of international and Indian brands. It's an ideal spot for serious shopping, with a mix of fashion, electronics, and home goods. The mall also features a multiplex cinema and numerous dining options.

Why visit: Perfect for a comprehensive shopping experience with a wide selection of brands and entertainment options, fitting your 'shopping' trip type.

Distance from previous: 14.5 km | Travel time: 45 minutes by taxi

  • Shopping for clothing, accessories, and electronics
  • Catching a movie at the multiplex
  • Exploring various food courts and restaurants
Practical Info:
  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Estimated Cost: Varies based on shopping
  • Best Time: Morning to avoid crowds, or evening for entertainment
  • Duration: 3-4 hours
  • Accessibility: Wheelchair accessible, elevators and escalators available
Tips:
  • What to bring: Comfortable walking shoes, Reusable shopping bag
  • What to buy: Fashion apparel, Souvenirs
  • Safety Notes: Keep an eye on your belongings in crowded areas
  • Customs/Etiquette: Bargaining is generally not practiced in large malls
  • Photo Spots: Grand entrances, Decorative installations inside the mall

Afternoon: Commercial Street

Commercial Street is a bustling shopping hub in Bangalore, renowned for its diverse offerings from street-side stalls to boutique stores. You can find everything from traditional Indian wear, jewelry, and handicrafts to modern clothing and accessories. It's a vibrant area that provides a true local shopping experience.

Why visit: A fantastic destination for budget-friendly shopping and experiencing the local retail culture, aligning with your 'budget-friendly' and 'shopping' interests.

Distance from previous: 10.0 km | Travel time: 30 minutes by taxi

  • Bargaining for goods at street stalls
  • Browsing unique jewelry and handicraft shops
  • Trying local street food
Practical Info:
  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Estimated Cost: Varies based on shopping
  • Best Time: Afternoon for vibrant atmosphere
  • Duration: 2-3 hours
  • Accessibility: Challenging for wheelchairs due to crowds and uneven pavements
Tips:
  • What to bring: Cash (for street vendors), Water bottle, Sunscreen
  • What to buy: Ethnic clothing, Costume jewelry, Handicrafts
  • Safety Notes: Be aware of pickpockets in crowded areas, Check quality of goods before purchasing
  • Customs/Etiquette: Bargaining is expected here
  • Photo Spots: Colorful shop displays, Bustling street scenes
View on Map

Evening: UB City Mall

UB City Mall is India's first luxury mall, offering a high-end shopping and dining experience. It houses international luxury brands, fine dining restaurants, and art galleries. The architecture itself is impressive, making it a great spot for window shopping and enjoying a sophisticated evening.

Why visit: Offers a different facet of shopping, with luxury brands and a refined ambiance, perfect for an evening out with friends.

Distance from previous: 4.0 km | Travel time: 30 minutes by auto-rickshaw/taxi

  • Window shopping at luxury boutiques
  • Enjoying a gourmet dinner
  • Experiencing the upscale atmosphere
Practical Info:
  • Entry Fee: Free
  • Estimated Cost: High for purchases/dining
  • Best Time: Evening for dining and ambiance
  • Duration: 2-3 hours
  • Accessibility: Fully wheelchair accessible
Tips:
  • What to bring: Dress smart casual
  • What to buy: Luxury goods (if budget permits), High-end fashion
  • Safety Notes: Secure environment
  • Customs/Etiquette: Dress appropriately for a luxury establishment
  • Photo Spots: The Collection façade, Rooftop views from restaurants

Day 2: 2025-10-15 - Cultural Exploration & Modern Shopping

Optimized Route: Hotel Lounge Hotel → Lalbagh Botanical Garden → Vidhana Soudha → Bangalore Palace → Orion Mall → Hotel Lounge Hotel

Total Day Distance: 30.2 km | Total Travel Time: 2 hours 10 minutes

Morning: Lalbagh Botanical Garden

Lalbagh Botanical Garden is a historic garden and a significant landmark in Bengaluru, famous for its rare collection of tropical plants, a beautiful glass house, and a serene lake. It's a perfect escape from the city's hustle, offering lush greenery and a tranquil environment.

Why visit: Provides a peaceful start to the day and a glimpse into Bengaluru's natural beauty and historical significance, fitting your 'city exploration' interest.

Distance from previous: 2.0 km | Travel time: 10 minutes by auto-rickshaw

  • Strolling through the gardens
  • Visiting the Glass House
  • Boating on the lake
Practical Info:
  • Entry Fee: INR 20-30 per person
  • Estimated Cost: INR 50-100 (entry + small refreshments)
  • Best Time: Early morning for pleasant weather
  • Duration: 2-3 hours
  • Accessibility: Partially wheelchair accessible, some paths are uneven
Tips:
  • What to bring: Camera, Water bottle, Hat/sunglasses
  • What to buy: Botanical-themed souvenirs (if available)
  • Safety Notes: Stay on marked paths
  • Customs/Etiquette: Respect the flora and fauna, no littering
  • Photo Spots: Glass House, Flowerbeds, Ancient trees
View on Map

Late Morning: Vidhana Soudha

Vidhana Soudha is the seat of the state legislature of Karnataka. This magnificent building is a prime example of neo-Dravidian architecture, showcasing intricate carvings and grand design. While entry inside is restricted, its external grandeur makes it a must-see landmark.

Why visit: An iconic architectural marvel and a symbol of democratic governance, offering a historical and cultural insight into Bengaluru.

Distance from previous: 4.5 km | Travel time: 20 minutes by auto-rickshaw

  • Admiring the architecture from outside
  • Taking photographs of the grand edifice
Practical Info:
  • Entry Fee: Free (external viewing)
  • Estimated Cost: Free
  • Best Time: Daytime for clear views
  • Duration: 30 minutes - 1 hour
  • Accessibility: Grounds are accessible
Tips:
  • What to bring: Camera
  • What to buy:
  • Safety Notes: Photography of the building's interior is prohibited
  • Customs/Etiquette: Maintain decorum as it's a government building
  • Photo Spots: Front facade of Vidhana Soudha
View on Map

Afternoon: Bangalore Palace

Bangalore Palace is a sprawling royal residence, built in the Tudor Revival style, reminiscent of England's Windsor Castle. It features exquisite wooden carvings, Gothic windows, and vibrant paintings, offering a glimpse into the opulent lifestyle of the Wodeyar dynasty. The palace grounds also host various events and concerts.

Why visit: Explore a royal heritage site with impressive architecture and historical significance, adding a cultural dimension to your city exploration.

Distance from previous: 3.5 km | Travel time: 15 minutes by auto-rickshaw

  • Touring the palace interiors
  • Exploring the palace grounds
  • Attending any ongoing exhibitions or events
Practical Info:
  • Entry Fee: INR 230 for Indians, INR 460 for foreigners (approx.)
  • Estimated Cost: INR 500 (entry + audio guide)
  • Best Time: Any time during operating hours
  • Duration: 1.5 - 2 hours
  • Accessibility: Limited accessibility for wheelchairs inside the palace
Tips:
  • What to bring: Camera (extra fees for photography/videography), Audio guide (available for rent)
  • What to buy: Souvenirs from the palace gift shop
  • Safety Notes: Be mindful of uneven surfaces inside the palace
  • Customs/Etiquette: Remove shoes before entering certain areas
  • Photo Spots: Palace facade, Interiors (if photography allowed with permit)

Evening: Orion Mall

Orion Mall is one of Bengaluru's premier shopping destinations, known for its extensive retail outlets, diverse food and beverage options, and an artificial lake that adds to its ambiance. It's a great place to enjoy a mix of shopping, dining, and leisure activities.

Why visit: Offers a modern shopping experience with dining and entertainment, perfect for wrapping up your day with friends.

Distance from previous: 8.5 km | Travel time: 35 minutes by taxi

  • Shopping at various retail stores

  • Enjoying a boat ride on the artificial lake
Practical Info:

  • Estimated Cost: Varies based on shopping and dining
  • Best Time: Evening for dining and lakeside views
  • Duration: 2-3 hours
  • Accessibility: Wheelchair accessible
Tips:
  • What to bring:
  • What to buy: Fashion, electronics, home goods
  • Safety Notes: Keep personal belongings secure
  • Customs/Etiquette: Normal mall etiquette
