Travel Tips for Europe

  • Learn a few basic phrases in local languages (e.g., "Bonjour", "Grazie").
  • Always validate your train/metro tickets to avoid fines.
  • Keep an eye on your belongings in crowded tourist areas to deter pickpockets.
  • Try local cuisine! Don't be afraid to step off the main tourist streets.
  • Public transport is generally excellent; consider city passes if doing lots of sightseeing.
  • Stay hydrated and carry a reusable water bottle.
