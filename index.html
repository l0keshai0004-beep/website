Day 3: 16 October 2025 - Historical Grandeur and Last-Minute Shopping

Route: Lusia Inn → Bangalore Palace → Forum Mall Koramangala → Lusia Inn

Total Distance: 23.4 km | Total Travel Time: 1h 22m

Morning: Bangalore Palace

Bangalore Palace is a magnificent royal palace built in the Tudor Revival style, reminiscent of England's Windsor Castle. It offers a glimpse into the opulent lifestyle of the Wodeyars, the erstwhile maharajas of Mysore. The palace grounds are also used for various events and concerts.

Why visit: A unique historical landmark providing insight into royal Indian architecture and history, perfect for cultural exploration.

Activities:
  • Explore the palace interiors and artifacts
  • Admire the architecture and intricate carvings
  • Stroll through the palace grounds
  • Learn about the history of the Wodeyar dynasty
Practical Info:
