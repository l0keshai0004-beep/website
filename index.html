Your Daily Adventure

Day 1: Arrival & Parisian Icons

🗓️ Day 1 - Paris: Eiffel Tower, Louvre

🗼 Eiffel Tower (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)

Ascend to the summit for breathtaking panoramic views of Paris. Pre-book tickets to save time!

  • Photo opportunities
  • Summit visit
  • Gift shop

Budget: €25 (Entry fee)

Map: View on Map

🖼️ Louvre Museum (2:00 PM - 5:30 PM)

Explore one of the world's largest and most famous art museums, home to masterpieces like the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo.

  • Mona Lisa viewing
  • Venus de Milo
  • Egyptian antiquities
  • European paintings

Budget: €20 (Entry fee)

Map: View on Map

🚗 Route: Hotel Le Littré → Eiffel Tower → Louvre
📏 Distance: Approx. 5 km
💰 Daily Budget: €80 (excluding hotel)
⏰ Time: 09:00-18:00 (Paris timezone)
Tips for Day 1:
  • Book Eiffel Tower and Louvre tickets online in advance to skip lines.
  • Wear comfortable shoes, as you'll be doing a lot of walking.
  • Consider a Parisian picnic near the Eiffel Tower for lunch.

Day 2: Parisian Charm & Montmartre

🗓️ Day 2 - Paris: Notre Dame, Latin Quarter, Montmartre

⛪ Notre Dame Cathedral (9:30 AM - 11:00 AM)

Admire the exterior of the iconic cathedral and explore its surroundings on Île de la Cité. Note: Interior access is limited due to ongoing restoration.

  • Exterior viewing
  • Walk around Île de la Cité
  • Pont Neuf views

Budget: Free (Exterior)

Map: View on Map

📚 Latin Quarter & Shakespeare and Company (11:30 AM - 1:30 PM)

Wander through the historic Latin Quarter, visit the famous English-language bookstore, and soak in the bohemian atmosphere.

  • Explore Rue Mouffetard
  • Visit Pantheon
  • Browse Shakespeare and Company
  • Lunch at a local bistro

Budget: €30 (Lunch & Pantheon entry)

Map: View on Map

🎨 Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur Basilica (3:00 PM - 6:00 PM)

Discover the artistic neighborhood of Montmartre, visit the beautiful Sacré-Cœur Basilica, and enjoy street artists at Place du Tertre.

  • Sacré-Cœur Basilica visit
  • Place du Tertre artists
  • Views of Paris
  • Walk through charming streets

Budget: €10 (Funicular/Souvenirs)

Map: View on Map

🚌 Route: Notre Dame → Latin Quarter (walk) → Montmartre (metro)
📏 Distance: Approx. 7 km (mixed travel)
💰 Daily Budget: €70 (excluding hotel)
⏰ Time: 09:30-19:00 (Paris timezone)
Tips for Day 2:
  • Take the funicular up to Sacré-Cœur to save your legs.
  • Beware of pickpockets in crowded tourist areas like Montmartre.
  • Enjoy dinner in Montmartre for a lively evening atmosphere.

Day 3: Versailles Grandeur & Parisian Markets

🗓️ Day 3 - Paris/Versailles: Palace of Versailles, Les Puces de Saint-Ouen

👑 Palace of Versailles (9:00 AM - 3:00 PM)

Take a day trip to the magnificent Palace of Versailles, explore its opulent interiors, and wander through the vast gardens.

  • Hall of Mirrors
  • Royal Apartments
  • Gardens of Versailles
  • Marie Antoinette's Estate

Budget: €25 (Entry fee), €10 (Train)

Map: View on Map

🛍️ Les Puces de Saint-Ouen (4:00 PM - 7:00 PM)

Immerse yourself in the world's largest antique market, a treasure trove of vintage finds, antiques, and unique curiosities.

  • Browse antique stalls
  • Hunt for vintage fashion
  • Enjoy street food
  • People-watching

Budget: €20 (Shopping/Snacks)

Map: View on Map

🚆 Route: Paris (train to Versailles) → Versailles → Paris (metro to Saint-Ouen)
📏 Distance: Approx. 40 km (round trip)
💰 Daily Budget: €75 (excluding hotel)
⏰ Time: 09:00-19:00 (Paris timezone)
Tips for Day 3:
  • Allocate plenty of time for Versailles, especially the gardens. Consider renting a golf cart.
  • Negotiate prices at the flea market.
  • Check market opening hours; they are typically weekends and Mondays.

Travel Tips for Europe

  • Learn a few basic phrases in local languages (e.g., "Bonjour", "Grazie").
  • Always validate your train/metro tickets to avoid fines.
  • Keep an eye on your belongings in crowded tourist areas to deter pickpockets.
  • Try local cuisine! Don't be afraid to step off the main tourist streets.
  • Public transport is generally excellent; consider city passes if doing lots of sightseeing.
  • Stay hydrated and carry a reusable water bottle.
