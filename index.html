💡 Travel Tips for Bangalore

  • Local Transport: Use ride-sharing apps like Ola or Uber for convenient and affordable travel. Auto-rickshaws are plentiful but negotiate fares or ensure meter usage.
  • Food & Water: Explore street food but choose busy stalls with fresh-cooked items. Stick to bottled water. Bangalore has a vibrant cafe culture – enjoy!
  • Bargaining: In local markets like Commercial Street or KR Market, bargaining is common and expected.
  • Connectivity: Consider getting a local SIM card or eSIM for data connectivity. Free Wi-Fi is available in many cafes and hotels.
  • Safety: Bangalore is generally safe, but like any big city, be aware of your surroundings, especially at night.
  • Weather: October in Bangalore is generally pleasant, but be prepared for occasional showers. Humidity can be high.
  • Language: Kannada is the local language, but English and Hindi are widely understood, especially in commercial areas.
